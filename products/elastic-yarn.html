ELASTIC YARN

Elastic Yarn is a stretchable, flexible yarn made by combining spandex or rubber with other fibers. It is widely used in garments that require stretch, like socks, waistbands, sportswear, and underwear.

Elastic Yarn is a highly stretchable and resilient yarn designed to provide elasticity and comfort in textiles. It is typically made by combining core materials like spandex (Lycra) or rubber with outer coverings such as polyester, nylon, or cotton through processes like air covering or mechanical wrapping.

This yarn is widely used in the textile industry for producing stretchable fabrics that retain shape and fit. Common applications include waistbands, cuffs, socks, leggings, sportswear, and intimate apparel. Its excellent stretch-recovery performance ensures long-lasting fit, freedom of movement, and enhanced garment durability.

Features

  • Available in multiple deniers
  • Wide colour options for diverse applications
  • High stretch with excellent recovery
  • Strong tensile strength
  • Heat & abrasion resistant
  • Smooth, skin-friendly finish
  • Uniform covering for consistency
  • Suitable for weaving, knitting & narrow fabrics
